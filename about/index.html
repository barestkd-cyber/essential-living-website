About us

A wellness clinic built for whole families

Essential Living Chiropractic exists to help people reach their full health potential without drugs or surgery, through quality chiropractic care delivered with loving service. That has been the mission since day one.

Meet Dr. Josh Nannen

Dr. Josh’s love of serving people and his passion for how the human body works drew him to chiropractic as a career. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Health and Wellness and a Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Parker University in Dallas. He is Webster Certified through the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA), a certification focused on chiropractic care during pregnancy.

He and his wife Nicole, who is from Tyler, along with their children Liam, Gavin, Melody, and Tobias, are building a community of health, wellness, and family at Essential Living Chiropractic.

Meet Dr. Danielle Lewis

Dr. Danielle Lewis is a chiropractor at Essential Living, and she is Webster Certified through the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA). She cares for patients and families with a gentle, thorough approach.

How we think about health

Your nervous system runs everything: your heartbeat, your breathing, your digestion, the reflex that pulls your hand off a hot stove. When life piles on stress, physical, chemical, or emotional, that stress builds up in the body and interferes with how the nervous system functions.

Chiropractic care removes that interference with gentle, specific adjustments, so the body can do what it was designed to do: heal itself. We value God and family, and strive to have integrity in all we do.

Whatever health goals you walk in with, we will meet you where you are and do everything in our power to help you reach them.
