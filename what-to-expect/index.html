Your first visit

What to expect

No mystery, no pressure. Here is exactly how a first visit goes.

  1. Quick paperwork

    Once your appointment is set, complete your intake forms online before you arrive, or come 10 minutes early and do them here.

  2. Consultation

    Dr. Josh sits down with you, hears what is going on, and learns your health history and goals.

  3. Assessment

    A thorough evaluation of your spine and nervous system, so care is based on what your body shows, not guesswork.

  4. Your first adjustment

    When it is appropriate, you will get your first gentle adjustment the same day, with everything explained as he goes.

  5. Your plan

    You leave knowing what comes next and why. Clear recommendations, your choice, no pressure.
