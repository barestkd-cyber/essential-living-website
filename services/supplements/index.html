Our approach to nutrition

We carry the Standard Process line, whole-food based supplements used in chiropractic and wellness practices across the country. Recommendations come after a conversation about your health, your diet, and your goals, never off a shelf card.

Already taking supplements or medications? Bring a list. Knowing what's in the mix helps avoid overlap and wasted money.

Common questions

Do I have to buy supplements to be a patient?

No. Nutrition support is optional and always will be. Plenty of our patients never touch the supplement shelf, and their care doesn't change one bit.

Why whole-food supplements?

Whole-food supplements are made from concentrated foods rather than isolated synthetic ingredients. If you want the deeper explanation, ask Dr. Josh, it's a favorite topic.
