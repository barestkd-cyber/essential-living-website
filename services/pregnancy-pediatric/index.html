Care during pregnancy

Pregnancy changes how the whole body carries itself. Weight shifts forward, ligaments loosen, and the low back and pelvis take on load they've never carried before. Many expecting moms deal with low back pain, hip discomfort, and restless sleep as a result.

Care at Essential Living is adapted trimester by trimester, with gentle techniques and positioning that keep mom comfortable and safe the entire visit.

Care for kids

A child's adjustment looks nothing like an adult's. The pressure used on an infant is about what you'd use to test a ripe tomato, and sessions for kids are quick, gentle, and matched to their age and size.

Essential Living is a member practice of the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA), and our office is built so kids feel at home, because half the battle with little ones is making the visit fun.

Common questions

Is chiropractic care safe during pregnancy?

Chiropractic care during pregnancy is common, and every technique Dr. Josh uses with expecting moms is adapted for pregnancy. We also encourage you to keep your OB or midwife in the loop. Good care teams talk to each other.

When during pregnancy can I start?

Moms come to us at every stage. Dr. Josh will tailor care to exactly where you are, and the first visit always starts with a conversation about your pregnancy and your goals.

Why would a baby or child need an adjustment?

Parents bring kids in for all kinds of reasons: a checkup after birth, comfort, posture as they grow, or because chiropractic care is already part of the family's routine. Care is always gentle and age-appropriate, and Dr. Josh will tell you honestly what he finds.

Can I stay with my child during the visit?

Always. You're with them the whole time, and Dr. Josh explains everything as he goes, to you and to them.
