How it works

Whole-body vibration platforms send gentle, rapid pulses through the body that muscles respond to reflexively. Used alongside an adjustment, a few guided minutes on the plate helps the body engage and balance.

Common questions

What does it feel like?

A gentle buzz through your feet and legs. Most patients find it pleasant, and sessions last just a few minutes.

Is it right for everyone?

Not always, and Dr. Josh will tell you if it isn't a fit for your situation. It's one tool among several, never a requirement.
